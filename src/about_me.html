About Me

I graduated from Universitat Pompeu Fabra in Barcelona, Spain with an MSc in Bioinformatics. Before that, I studied Neuroscience at Bates College.

I am currently a Scientist at Stylus Medicine working on next-generation gene therapies for cancer. Previously, I worked on engineered tumor-infiltrating lymphocyte (TIL) therapies at KSQ Therapeutics.

Outside of work, I play guitar and make indie folk music.
